Booking rules

  • Every launch is tied to a rocket with pricing and minimum passengers.
  • Rocket capacity limits available seats for each booking request.
  • Launch lifecycle moves from scheduled to confirmed to successful.
  • Customers are identified by email, name, and phone number.
  • Customers can book multiple seats without exceeding availability.
  • Payments are charged at booking time through a mock gateway.

API

Base URL: http://localhost:3000

Resources: rockets, launches, customers, bookings

AstroBookings is fictional and this app is for training only.
